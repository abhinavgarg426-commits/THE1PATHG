Our Mission

The Hindu Path exists to make the profound wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita, Upanishads, and Sacred Shlokas accessible to every seeker on the planet — completely free, beautifully presented, and available in multiple languages including Sanskrit, English, and Hindi.

"कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन। मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि।"

"You have the right to perform your prescribed duties, but you are not entitled to the fruits of your actions."

What's Inside

The Bhagavad Gita — the 700-verse scripture spoken by Lord Krishna to Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra — is the essence of all Vedic knowledge. The Upanishads are its philosophical foundation, exploring the nature of Brahman, the Atman (soul), and the ultimate reality behind all existence. The Shlokas and chants are sacred sounds that have been sung and recited for millennia, transforming consciousness and opening doors to the infinite.

ॐ Bhagavad Gita

18 chapters covering Karma Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, Jnana Yoga, and the path of Sannyasa. The complete manual for living with purpose.

ॐ Upanishads

10 Principal Upanishads including Isha, Kena, Katha, Mundaka, and Mandukya — the end of knowledge, the highest wisdom.

ॐ Sacred Shlokas

Gayatri, Mahamrityunjaya, Om Namah Shivaya and more — chants of power, protection, and divine transformation.
